Science - Plant Growth Experiment

Oct 28, 2023

The hands-on observation activity worked very well. Students were engaged in measuring and recording plant growth daily. However, some groups needed more guidance in accurate measurement techniques. Next time I'll include a demonstration of proper measurement methods before starting.

Math - Multiplication Concepts

Oct 21, 2023

The multiplication bingo game was highly effective for reinforcing facts. Students who typically struggle showed improvement. Need to create differentiated versions for varying skill levels. The visual array method worked well for concrete understanding - will use more visual aids in future lessons.
